RIALO GAME GUIDE

How Rialo PM Works

Build your prediction from the group stage to the final. Rank every group, generate the knockout bracket, choose your champion, set your RLO entry, and submit your complete picks on Rialo.

01

Rank Groups

Start with 12 groups. Move teams up or down to predict the final ranking of each group. Lock every group when your prediction is ready.

02

Generate Bracket

After all groups are locked, the game creates a 32-team knockout bracket from your rankings, including qualified third-place teams.

03

Pick Winners

Select winners round by round until the final. Your bracket progress updates automatically as you complete each prediction.

04

RLO Entry & Submit

Enter your RLO amount, check the odds and potential return, then submit on Rialo when all groups, bracket picks, and champion selection are complete.

05

Prize Pool Access

Once you participate by predicting the World Cup winner, whether your prediction wins or loses, you will enter the 120,000 RLO Prize Pool. The distribution will take place after the World Cup Final on Sunday, July 19, 2026.

06

Smart Winning System

The winning system does not require your predictions to be 100% correct. Rewards are based on how close your bracket predictions are to the real results. For example, if you correctly predict one team in the quarter-finals, you will receive a certain share. If you correctly predict three teams, your reward share increases, and so on. The closer your predictions are, the higher your potential reward.

Higher-risk champion picks can increase your odds. Favorites are safer but give lower returns.
